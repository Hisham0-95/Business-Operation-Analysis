# Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive, end-to-end analysis of Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and customer service operations. By bridging the gap between workforce availability and agent performance, this dashboard empowers operational leaders to identify bottlenecks, optimize staffing, and monitor adherence to service level targets.

# Business Objectives

**Monitor Workforce Availability:** Identify trends in absenteeism, sickness, and overall schedule adherence to ensure optimal staffing levels.

**Evaluate Channel Performance:** Compare Actual Average Handle Time (AHT) against channel-specific targets across Phone, Email, and Chat interactions.

**Measure Productivity:** Accurately track how agents spend their logged-in time through Utilization and Occupancy metrics.

# Key Metrics & KPIs

**Shrinkage & Attendance Metrics**

Absenteeism %: Calculates the ratio of total absent days against total scheduled days.

Unplanned Shrinkage %: Measures unexpected time off (Total Unplanned Shrinkage Days ÷ Scheduled Days).

Planned Shrinkage %: Tracks approved/expected time off (Total Planned Shrinkage Days ÷ Total Days).

Sickness %: A specialized metric isolating sickness trends using the formula (Sick + Planned Sick) ÷ (Scheduled Days + Planned Sick).

**Productivity & Performance Metrics**

AHT: Weighted AHT targets based on contact volume across distinct channels

Utilization %: The percentage of scheduled shift time agents spend in productive states (Busy, Available, Task, Email) versus total logged-in duration.

Occupancy %: Measures how intensely agents are working while logged in by comparing active handle time against total available time.

**Performance & User Experience**

User Experience: Evaluates overall customer satisfaction by calculating the ratio of positive experience scores against total valid responses (User Experience Numerator ÷ User Experience Denominator).

Professionalism %: Measures agent conduct, tone, and adherence to quality standards during interactions (Professionalism Numerator ÷ Professionalism Denominator).

Resolution Rate: Tracks the percentage of customer issues successfully resolved, calculated as total resolved contacts divided by total eligible contacts (Resolution Rate Numerator ÷ Resolution Rate Denominator).

Knowledge Score: Assesses the agent's product and process expertise demonstrated during the interaction (Knowledge Numerator ÷ Knowledge Denominator).

# Insights


1- **Unplanned By Company:** India is currently facing critical attendance challenges, driven by a 15.44% Unplanned Shrinkage rate. This is further compounded by 5.72% Absenteeism and 7.49% Sickness.

2- **Attendance By Country:** Mahindra& Mahindra has the highest unplanned shrinkage rate, with 16.48% and 0% planned shrinkage.


3- **Overstaff Observation:** The gap between Occupancy % (Time spent working vs. waiting) and Utilization % (Time spent working vs. paid shift) is widening.

4- **Ferrari User Experience:** Ferrari has a very low EX, 50% of the customers were not satisfied after contacting Ferrari.

# SUMMARY & STRATEGIC OUTLOOK

**Phase 1: Diagnosis (What We Did)**

Established a Single Source of Truth: Deployed three integrated Command Centers covering Availability, Efficiency, and Quality.

Identified Critical Leakage: Pinpointed systemic shrinkage variance in India and the "Substitution Effect" (Planned vs. Unplanned shift).

Quantified Efficiency Gaps: Confirmed overstaffing as the primary driver of low CPH (3.29) and high Idle Time.

Uncovered Process Gaps: Detected a "Transfer Paradox" where high-knowledge agents are bypassing resolution protocols.



**Phase 2: Execution (Focus for Next Period)**

Stabilize Availability: Launch targeted PIPs for outlier Team Leaders and the Perfect Attendance Bonus pilot.

Optimize Cost: (WFM) will review the interval arrival patterns and reduce headcount during these specific quiet windows.

Elevate Experience: Deploy the "Ferrari Tiger Team" and initiate the cross-channel (Phone To  Email) mentorship program.
